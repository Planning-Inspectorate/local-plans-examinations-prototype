Comment on the Local Plan

You can use this service to make a formal representation on the Local Plan under Regulation 19 of the Town and Country Planning (Local Planning) (England) Regulations 2012.

Representations must relate to the legal compliance or soundness of the plan. You can also comment on whether the plan complies with the duty to cooperate.

Your representation will be submitted to the Planning Inspectorate and may be published. Please do not include personal or sensitive information unless necessary.

  • Comment on specific paragraphs, policies or map references
  • Upload supporting documents
  • Request to participate in examination hearings

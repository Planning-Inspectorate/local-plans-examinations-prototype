You may not need every document listed - this depends on how complex your plan is. Provide as much as you can to get the most useful feedback from your Gateway 2 assessor.

Document What is needed
Gateway 2 cover letter Overview of progress, key issues for advice and list of submitted documents.
Local Plan timetable Current timetable showing planned and actual milestone dates.
Project Initiation Document Project management arrangements.
Draft Statement of Compliance Early evidence of progress towards meeting prescribed requirements.
Draft Statement of Soundness Potential soundness issues for discussion.
Notice of Intention to commence local plan preparation Confirmation of when plan-making began and the area the plan will cover.
Scoping consultation documents Documents used to consult on the scope of the plan's proposed content and evidence.
Consultation Summary of Feedback to Scoping Consultation Summary of the responses received to the scoping consultation.
Gateway 1 - Self Assessment of Readiness Self-assessment confirming readiness to begin preparing the plan.
Consultation on proposed local plan content and evidence documents Documents used to consult on the proposed content and evidence base for the plan.
Consultation summary for the above Summary of the responses received to that consultation.

Subsequent work towards a draft plan

Document What is needed
Topic papers Papers on the five issues identified by the LPA, each within a 2,000 word limit
Full list of evidence base documents A complete list of evidence base documents, with target dates for completion of any still in progress
Draft vision, aims and objectives, 10 draft outcomes Draft vision, aims and objectives for the plan, plus the 10 draft outcomes
Spatial strategy options or draft spatial strategy Either the spatial strategy options under consideration, or a draft spatial strategy if one has been settled on
Latest version of a draft plan and/or policies map The most up-to-date draft plan and/or policies map available, even though neither is a mandatory Gateway 2 requirement
Progress towards setting a housing requirement Evidence of progress, such as a housing needs assessment and strategic housing market assessment
Progress towards identifying Traveller accommodation needs Evidence of progress, such as an accommodation assessment
Progress towards setting other development needs (employment, retail, etc.) Evidence of progress on identifying needs and supply for other development types
Site selection approach/evidence Evidence of the approach taken to selecting sites
Draft Green Belt review A draft of the Green Belt review, where relevant to the plan
Evidence relevant to matters of soundness to be discussed Evidence covering the specific soundness matters the LPA wants the assessor's advice on
Evidence on engagement between plan-making authorities/relevant bodies Evidence demonstrating engagement with other plan-making authorities and relevant bodies
Progress on Habitats Regulations and Environmental Assessment Regulations assessments Evidence of progress on assessments required under both sets of regulations

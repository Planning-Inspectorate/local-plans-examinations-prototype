Notes:

  • Gateways presented in a tasklist view with statuses (Completed, In progress, Cannot start yet)
  • Content includes key information such as descriptions, dates and links to relevant actions
  • Focus on reducing uncertainty around readiness and whether users can proceed

Things to test:

  • Do users understand what "new" or “in progress” means at each gateway?
  • Is it clear why some stages cannot be started yet?
  • Can users easily identify their next action from this page?
  • Do users understand the role of PINS at different gateways?
  • Is the difference between gateways clear without needing additional guidance?
  • What metadata is useful to provide on this page?
  • Do users need access to their gateway 1 documents from here?
  • Whether users understand what to do in each state (for example, ready to start, in progress, with case officer, or information required)

Open questions:

  • Do users need more explicit feedback on missing or incomplete materials?
  • Should stop/go decisions be more prominent or visually distinct?
  • Is additional guidance needed to explain what each gateway involves?
  • Would a more visual progress indicator (for example a progress bar) improve understanding?

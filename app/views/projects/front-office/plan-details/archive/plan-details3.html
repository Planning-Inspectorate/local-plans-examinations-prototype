What you need for Gateway 2

PINS will check that all required documents are present and correctly formatted. You must upload all documents listed below before submitting.

If your submission is complete, PINS will mark it valid and Gateway 3 will unlock. If anything is missing or incorrect, you will receive an email asking you to upload corrections and resubmit.

Required documents for Gateway 2:

  • Gateway 2 covering letter
  • Local plan timetable
  • Project initiation document
  • Draft statement of compliance
  • Draft statement of soundness
  • Consultation statement
  • Consultation summary for scoping consultation
  • Notice of intention to commence local plan preparation
{{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "How do the gateways differ?", html: "

Gateway 1 is a self-assessment. Your LPA confirms it is ready to proceed. PINS does not make a decision at this stage.

Gateway 2 is a completeness check by PINS. All required documents must be present and correctly formatted. A passed GW2 is a go decision to proceed to GW3.

Gateway 3 is a soundness and legal compliance check. PINS reviews whether the plan meets interim standards. A passed GW3 means your plan can proceed to examination.

Examination is conducted by an independent inspector who assesses the plan against the National Planning Policy Framework.
